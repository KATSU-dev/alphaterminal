The DigiROM

Digital Read Only Memory

The Digital Read Only Memory (DigiROM) is a text-based format that represents the communication output of your VPet device. It stores all of the data your device sends through its connection pins, and allows you to share it with others. DigiROMs can be sent back to your VPet device by loading it into Alpha Terminal and using the Send function. In Alpha Terminal the DigiROM codes are encrypted to prevent any cheating.
