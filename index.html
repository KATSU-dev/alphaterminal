Alpha Terminal

V-Pet-Computer Interfacing

Introduction

Alpha Terminal is a V-Pet interfacing project. Using an Android or Windows device, you can connect your V-Pet with other Alpha Terminal users by sharing DigiROM codes.

USB Interfacing

The Alpha-Com circuit interfaces your V-Pet's connection pins to the USB port on your Android or Windows device.

Anti-Cheat Encryption

DigiROM codes have been vulnerable to cheating via tampering with the data in the past. The Alpha Terminal program encrypts the data and checks for tampering. Users can share DigiROMs without the threat of cheaters.

Cross-Platform

Android and Windows compatibility are the focus of development, though further platform adoption will come later.

Microcontroller Based System

The Alpha-Com is an Arduino-based electronic system that allows you to link your V-Pet's connection pins to your Android or Windows device via USB. The Alpha-Com's small circuit is easy to build, or you can use the original D-Com circuit or Digivice Helper.

Augmented Reality


Connecting your V-Pet to your Android Device via the Alpha-Com can bring them into a new augmented world.
